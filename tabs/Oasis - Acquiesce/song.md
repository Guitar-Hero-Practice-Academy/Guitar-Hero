## Release / Context Notes

- First appeared as a B-side on Oasis' "Some Might Say" single in 1995.
- Later opened the B-sides compilation The Masterplan in 1998.
- Became a live staple and fan favourite.
- A 2025 unplugged version was announced for the 30th-anniversary reissue of (What's the Story) Morning Glory?
- Common song note: the split lead vocal is part of the point of the arrangement, with Liam taking the verses and Noel taking the choruses.
